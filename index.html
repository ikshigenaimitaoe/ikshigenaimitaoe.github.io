ABOUT US. It'll blow your mind.

We organize multiple fun and educational events in MITAOE related to AI, expert talks, workshops, projects, bootcamps and more. We strive to lower the entry-barrier in the emerging field of AI/ML by providing opportunities to interact with leaders in industry and academia, and also a hands-on project-based learning experience.

Our Vision See for yourself.

ikshiGen-AI is here to help students succeed in the growing field of AI and ML. As these technologies reshape the world, we aim to be the launchpad for students at MITAOE who want to build their careers in this area. We believe in interactive learning and interactive projects. Whether students are interested in research, working in the industry, hardware or software, ikshiGen-AI offers the right support, guidance, and practical experience. Our vision is to create a strong community of AI enthusiasts and become the go-to place for anyone with questions or ideas in this field. Let's shape the future together!

Objective. Checkmate.

ikshiGen-AI, the Artificial Intelligence Club of MITAOE, is a community of passionate students eager to explore and apply AI. Our club is a hub for students who are excited about learning, sharing ideas, and using AI to solve both academic and real-world problems. ikshiGen-AI aims to bring together bright minds who are ready to push the boundaries of innovation, making it an ideal support for the future of AI enthusiasts.
